International Trips

Highlighting USF Honors College study abroad opportunities

The USF Honors College takes students every year to different countries around the world. Many of the trips are extensions of Honors classes where students learn about the country and culture beforehand, and then have a first-hand learning experience.

My Role

Interview students who went on the Honors trips and blend their responses, pictures, and videos to create a video to promote the trip to future students.

Honors Faculty Bios

Short interview-style videos highlighting USF Honors College faculty members

The Honors College faculty are an impressive bunch of hard-working and extremely intelligent, creative, and insightful individuals. This series of videos shares the educational backgrounds of the instructors, classes they teach at the Honors College, and a parting wisdom they would like to instill in their students.

My Role

I initiated this video series and came up with questions, filmed, and edited 9 faculty bio videos

Small Shoes, Big Journey

Highlighting the "Tampa Bay" Welcoming Event

Every year, USF hosts a national "Welcoming Week" event to embrace the contributions of refugees and immigrants to American communities. The theme this year was Small Shoes, Big Journey to embrace the children that now call Tampa home.

My Role

I volunteered all day to capture the event and all of the various activities held throughout the day. I then edited the footage together to create this montage to help showcase the diversity of the Tampa Bay community, as well as promote this amazing event in years to come.

USF FRAS 2016

Showcasing USF Resident Assistants for their annual FL RA Conference

The Florida Resident Assistant Seminar (FRAS) is a three day event held each year in a Florida school. The 2016 seminar was hosted by the Florida Institute of Technology in Melbourne, Florida, and is aimed to showcase the work that Residential Assistants (RA's) do on their respective campuses.

My Role

I was recruited to make a promotional video about the USF-Tampa RA's on the recommendation of Greg Bowers, the USF Housing and Residential Assistant Director. I filmed and edited this short film in accordance to the 2016 FRAS theme - boardgames.
