USF FRAS 2016

Showcasing USF Resident Assistants for their annual FL RA Conference

The Florida Resident Assistant Seminar (FRAS) is a three day event held each year in a Florida school. The 2016 seminar was hosted by the Florida Institute of Technology in Melbourne, Florida, and is aimed to showcase the work that Residential Assistants (RA's) do on their respective campuses.

My Role

I was recruited to make a promotional video about the USF-Tampa RA's on the recommendation of Greg Bowers, the USF Housing and Residential Assistant Director. I filmed and edited this short film in accordance to the 2016 FRAS theme - boardgames.
