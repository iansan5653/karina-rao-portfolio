International Trips

Highlighting USF Honors College study abroad opportunities.

The USF Honors College takes students every year to different countries around the world. Many of the trips are extensions of Honors classes where students learn about the country and culture beforehand, and then have a first-hand learning experience.

My Role

Interview students who went on the Honors trips and blend their responses, pictures, and videos to create a video to promote the trip to future students.
